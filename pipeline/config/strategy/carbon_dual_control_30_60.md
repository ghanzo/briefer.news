# Carbon Dual Control — 碳达峰碳中和 / 双控

## Origin

Xi Jinping's September 22, 2020 UN General Assembly address committed China to **carbon peak before 2030 and carbon neutrality by 2060** — "30/60" in Chinese energy-policy shorthand. Codified into the 14FYP (March 2021) and operationalized via the **"1+N" policy framework**: the State Council's October 2021 head document (the "1") plus N sectoral implementation plans across energy, industry, transport, agriculture, etc.

The original "dual control" (双控) target was energy consumption intensity + total consumption; reformed in 2022-2024 toward emissions-intensity + total-emissions caps, signaling a shift from blunt energy quotas to carbon-specific metering.

## Core themes

- **Capacity buildout as the leading instrument.** Not phase-out of coal, but accelerated buildout of non-fossil capacity. Target: 1,200 GW combined solar + wind by 2030 — already hit ahead of schedule (2024 actual ~1,200 GW; revised target raised).
- **Nuclear expansion.** Hualong One (HPR1000) standardized fleet build; CAP1400 / CGN; floating reactors. Target 70-80 GW operational by 2035 from ~57 GW in 2024.
- **Coal as system floor, not zero.** Coal-fired capacity continues to expand modestly as renewable-firming backstop; framed as "transition fuel" through 2030.
- **Grid as bottleneck.** UHV (ultra-high-voltage) transmission build to move renewable power from west/north to demand centers in the east/south.
- **National Carbon Trading Market** (launched July 2021) — expanding from power sector to industry; price signal still modest but rising.
- **EV / battery / solar manufacturing as economic strategy.** Climate ambition + industrial dominance + employment policy fused into one program.

## Current implementation focus

- **April 2026 export composition signal:** EVs +68%, lithium batteries +43%, wind turbines +41% YoY — the industrial side of 30/60 paying off in trade numbers.
- **State Council services-economy push** (Doc. No. 7, April 2026) treats energy-efficiency services as a growth sector.
- **Beautiful China cadre-assessment** (May 7, 2026) ties provincial-leader promotion to environmental performance — political-incentive layer for 30/60 enforcement.
- **15FYP drafting** is preparing the successor policy framework; expect tightening of carbon-intensity targets and broader emissions-cap rollout.
