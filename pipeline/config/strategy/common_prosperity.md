# Common Prosperity — 共同富裕

## Origin

Xi Jinping's August 17, 2021 Central Finance Committee speech elevated 共同富裕 from a long-standing Party slogan to an operational policy frame. The 14FYP (separate doc) was already in flight; common prosperity became its social-policy spine. Zhejiang province was named the demonstration zone (浙江高质量发展建设共同富裕示范区).

The 2021-2022 tech-platform crackdown (Didi, Alibaba, Meituan, Tencent gaming) was widely interpreted as a common-prosperity enforcement campaign — though Beijing has consistently emphasized regulation rather than rupture.

## Core themes

- **Distribution, not just growth.** Three-stage distribution: market (primary), redistribution (secondary, taxes/transfers), charity (tertiary, "voluntary" wealth contribution).
- **Regulating excessive wealth and disorderly capital expansion** (无序资本扩张). Antimonopoly, antitrust, platform regulation.
- **"Both material and spiritual wealth."** Cultural / ideological dimension — limits on celebrity culture, online gaming for minors, after-school tutoring (双减 policy).
- **Not egalitarianism, not welfarism.** Xi has repeatedly insisted this — common prosperity is gradual, market-compatible, not redistributive shock therapy.
- **Rural revitalization** (乡村振兴) as a key vehicle — narrowing urban-rural gap.

## Current implementation focus

By 2024-2025, common prosperity became less rhetorically prominent (some interpret as policy softening; others as quiet operationalization). 2026 indicators:
- Continued platform-algorithm regulation (CAC negative list, Meituan/Didi/Taobao changes).
- Beautiful China cadre-assessment scorecard (May 7, 2026) ties cadre promotion to environmental performance — common-prosperity adjacent.
- Services-economy expansion (State Council Doc. No. 7) — domestic services as broad-based growth.
- Anti-corruption (CCDI activity) — political-economic discipline framing.

## Today's coverage triggers

Bullets touching: tech-platform regulation, antitrust, algorithm rules, platform-worker protections (gig economy), wealth tax or property tax probes, rural revitalization, common-prosperity demonstration zone updates (Zhejiang), education policy (双减), online gaming / celebrity regulation, services-economy expansion, regional development gap, CCP discipline / anti-corruption against business interests.
