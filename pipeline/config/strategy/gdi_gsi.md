# Global Development / Security / Civilization Initiatives — GDI, GSI, GCI

## Origin

Three initiatives Xi Jinping introduced sequentially, designed to articulate a coherent alternative framework to the US-led international order:

- **Global Development Initiative (GDI, 全球发展倡议)** — Xi UN General Assembly speech, September 21, 2021.
- **Global Security Initiative (GSI, 全球安全倡议)** — Xi Boao Forum keynote, April 21, 2022.
- **Global Civilization Initiative (GCI, 全球文明倡议)** — Xi CCP in Dialogue with World Political Parties High-Level Meeting, March 15, 2023.

Together they form Beijing's positive-framing rebuttal to "containment" and "decoupling" narratives — Beijing as proponent of multilateralism, sovereignty, development-as-right, peaceful coexistence.

## Core themes

### GDI — Global Development Initiative
- Re-center the international agenda on development (UN SDGs as anchor).
- Eight priority areas: poverty reduction, food security, COVID/pandemic response, development financing, climate / green transition, industrialization, digital economy, connectivity.
- Operationalized via the Group of Friends of the GDI at the UN (~70+ members).

### GSI — Global Security Initiative
- "Common, comprehensive, cooperative, sustainable" security.
- Sovereignty + territorial integrity + non-interference as core principles.
- Six commitments including respect for the UN Charter, taking other countries' "legitimate security concerns" seriously (this clause = NATO/Russia framing).
- Dispute-resolution mediation as activity (Saudi-Arabia/Iran rapprochement March 2023 cited as a GSI exemplar).

### GCI — Global Civilization Initiative
- Civilizational diversity, mutual learning, opposition to "civilizational superiority" (read: Western liberal universalism).
- "Common values" (共同价值) — peace, development, fairness, justice, democracy, freedom — phrased to claim universalism while contesting Western definitions.

## Current implementation focus

- **MFA / spokesperson invocation.** GDI/GSI/GCI cited routinely in regular press conferences when commenting on UN, multilateralism, US bloc-building, AUKUS, Indo-Pacific frameworks.
- **BRICS expansion** (Egypt, Ethiopia, Iran, UAE, Saudi Arabia joined Jan 2024) — positioned as GDI/GSI multilateral instantiation.
- **Mediation roles** — Saudi-Iran (2023), attempts on Russia-Ukraine, Israel-Palestine peace conferencing (2024-2025).
- **UN Group of Friends** activity at General Assembly each September.
- **SCO + FOCAC + China-CELAC + China-Arab summit** processes as GDI-aligned multilateral fora.
