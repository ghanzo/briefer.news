# 14th Five-Year Plan — 第十四个五年规划

## Origin

Ratified by the 13th NPC in March 2021 alongside a longer-range "Vision 2035." First explicit Xi-era FYP after the 19th Party Congress (2017). Distinguished from prior plans by combining a 5-year operational plan with a 15-year strategic vision.

## Core themes

- **Dual circulation** (双循环, separate doc) — first elevated to top-line economic doctrine.
- **High-quality development** (高质量发展) — explicit pivot away from GDP-headline-rate growth toward productivity, innovation, environmental quality.
- **Common prosperity** (共同富裕, separate doc) — codified as the social-policy frame.
- **Tech self-reliance** in core sectors. Set the stage for what becomes "new quality productive forces."
- **Carbon dual control**: peak emissions before 2030, carbon neutrality by 2060 (the "30/60" targets). Domestic-energy and grid policy through 2025 anchors here.
- **People-first urbanization** (新型城镇化) and modernization of the agricultural sector.
- **Population long-term strategy** introduced — though the actual demographic contraction surprised even Beijing.

## Current implementation focus

The 14FYP is closing — 2025-2026 are wind-down. Most coverage in 2026 reflects either (a) **final-year evaluation**, (b) **carryover items into 15FYP**, or (c) **stocktaking** through Qiushi / People's Daily commentaries. Carbon targets remain operational and continue to drive industrial / energy policy.

## Today's coverage triggers

Bullets touching: high-quality development, carbon targets (30/60), urbanization, agricultural modernization, dual circulation, common prosperity, current Five-Year-Plan-final-year evaluations, GDP growth quality, energy transition, COP commitments.
